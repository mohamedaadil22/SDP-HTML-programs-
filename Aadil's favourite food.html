SOME OF MY FAVOURITE FOODS

GULAB JAMUNS

Gulab Jamuns is a Indian sweet which has a soft brown layer outside and soft layer inside they are mostly prefered in festival times

BARBECUE

Barbecue is a delicious dish which I mostly prefer everytime,each countries has their own style of Barbecues

PANI PURI

Pani puri is a North-Indian snack which is mostly prefered to be taken in the evening time and its my favourite snack

BUTTER NAAN

Butter naan is an Indian bread which is seasoned with some butter on top and they can be taken with any gravies for brunch and even dinner
